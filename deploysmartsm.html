Smart-SM

  • Smart-SM used for entering orders, collection and activities of sales man
  • here you can add order items quantity and rate details, images of order form also can be photographed.
  • you can add collection amount, cheque details and cheque images also

    • To install Smart-SM App follow these instructions

  1. First tap "Install Smart-SM" button.
  2. Followed by confirmation alert "Open this page in App Store?" tap open.
  3. Followed by installation alert "dl.dropboxusercontent.com would like to install Smart-SM" tap install.
  4. App start to install.
  5. once the instalation is complete tap the Smart-SM icon to open the app.
